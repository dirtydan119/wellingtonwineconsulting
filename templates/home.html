Services.

Hire a Sommelier
Wine Consulting

Private Dinner

Take the responsibility of wine selection and service out of your hands. We assist you to curate wine pairings specifically to your special evening menu selections. We provide full dinner services to engage every guest in the culinary and wine adventure.


Cellar Management

Wine fridge or a dedicated cellar? We’ll create trackable, monthly inventory records to keep your cellar alive and profitable in case you dont drink it all. We provide recommendations on what to buy and what is for drinking now and later.

Curated Wine Tasting

We offer the opportunity to explore why a wine tastes the way it does by breaking down it’s elements, natural and manipulated, to create understanding and confidence when talking wine. We’ll discuss the region it comes from, winemaking styles, soils, climatic impact and other variables that make each producer's wine unique...more

Rare and Vintage

In search of something rare? Perhaps a specific vintage for the birthday? An anniversary? A business deal? Whatever the occasion we do our best to search for a region and vintage that has a wine in prime drinking or aging condition.

Benjamin's Philosophy on Wine.

Driven by his passion for wines all over the world, Benjamin has taken every opportunity to travel. During his time in New York City, he traveled through famed wine regions of France and Italy to better grasp their culture. Benjamin chose to become a sommelier as a career because it encompasses all his other passions; food, travel, people, and nature! Wine is an essential aspect to all his passions because, let’s face it, without wine, dining and drinking would be much less interesting!
