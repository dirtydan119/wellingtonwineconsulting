Curated Wine Tasting

We offer the opportunity to explore why a wine tastes the way it does by breaking down it’s elements, natural and manipulated, to create understanding and confidence when talking wine. We’ll discuss the region it comes from, winemaking styles, soils, climatic impact and other variables that make each producer's wine unique.

We will answer familiar questions such as, why some wines are more or less drier or the influence of oak on certain varieties. We talk about reading the label, and why they are made so confusingly on some and other so simple. Exercises like blind tastings to highlight different examples helping you understand the myriad complexities of the same grape from different places. Food accompaniments available for the tasting!
